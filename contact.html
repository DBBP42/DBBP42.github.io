Frequently Asked Questions

Is DBBP a political party?

No. We are a horizontal network of citizens, not a traditional political party. We advocate for evidence-based policy reforms and support any political actors who genuinely work toward these goals.

Do I need to pay membership fees?

No. DBBP is not a membership organization with fees. We're a voluntary network united by shared values and vision.

How is DBBP funded?

Currently through voluntary contributions and donations. Our long-term vision includes transparent funding sources aligned with our reforms (such as cannabis taxation revenue directed to public benefit).

Can I attend events if I'm not Slovenian?

Absolutely! While our focus is on Slovenian reform, we welcome international visitors, researchers, and anyone interested in progressive policy models.

How do you make decisions without hierarchies?

We use consensus-based decision making, open forums, and distributed coordination. Our workshops on horizontal organization explain our model in detail.

Are you serious about the Hitchhiker's Guide references?

Yes and no. We use humor and pop culture references to make policy discussions more accessible, but our reform proposals are grounded in serious research and evidence. The absurdist philosophy reminds us not to take ourselves too seriously while working on serious problems.
