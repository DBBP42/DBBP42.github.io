Upcoming Events

Join us at our pan-galactic gatherings, policy workshops, and community events. Remember: Don't Panic, but do participate!

Annual Pan-Galactic Conference on Zero Tolerance

Date: Coming Soon

Location: Ljubljana, Slovenia

Description: Our flagship annual conference bringing together policy experts, activists, and concerned citizens to discuss comprehensive reforms for zero tolerance of sexual violence and systemic abuse. Features debates, workshops, and - of course - towels and Pan Galactic Gargle Blasters (the non-alcoholic kind, for safety).

Topics:

  • Prevention strategies and education
  • Effective prosecution and victim support
  • Institutional accountability
  • Creating a culture of consent

Commission 420 Youth Assembly

Date: Quarterly meetings

Location: Various locations across Slovenia

Description: Open forum for citizens under 35 to discuss substance policy reform, mental health initiatives, and the economics of cannabis legalization. This is where research meets reality, and young voices shape policy.

Who should attend: Young researchers, policy enthusiasts, advocates for drug reform, mental health professionals, and anyone interested in evidence-based approaches to substance regulation.

Horizontal Network Meetups

Frequency: Monthly

Location: Rotating locations (Ljubljana, Maribor, Koper, and more)

Description: Casual gatherings for DBBP members and interested citizens. No hierarchies, no formal presentations - just open discussion about how to build a better Slovenia. Bring your ideas, your questions, and your towel.

Format: Round-table discussions, collaborative brainstorming, and network building.

Universal Basic Income Town Halls

Date: TBA - Following pilot program launch

Location: Five pilot municipalities

Description: Community forums to discuss the implementation and impact of the UBI pilot program. Hear from participants, ask questions, and learn about the real-world effects of basic income.

Goal: Transparent communication and data-driven assessment of UBI outcomes.

Mental Health & Psychedelic Research Symposium

Date: Bi-annual

Location: University partners across Slovenia

Description: Academic and clinical professionals present latest research on psychedelic-assisted therapies for PTSD, depression, and addiction. Open to researchers, clinicians, students, and interested public.

Featured topics:

  • Psilocybin for depression (79% remission rates)
  • MDMA for PTSD (67% symptom-free outcomes)
  • Ketamine for suicidality (70% immediate effect)
  • Individual therapeutic approaches and safety protocols

Balkan Unity Forum

Date: Annual

Location: Alternating between Ljubljana, Zagreb, and Podgorica

Description: Regional gathering exploring economic integration, shared resource management, and cooperative governance across Slovenia, Croatia, and Montenegro. Building bridges for a prosperous Balkan future.

Vision: Shared market, energy cooperation, tourism development, and cultural exchange.

Workshop Series

Our ongoing educational workshops are free and open to all:

Understanding the 9 Reforms

Deep-dive workshops on each of our nine reform pillars. Learn the research, the economics, and the implementation roadmap.

Duration: 2-hour sessions, one reform per session

Registration: See contact page

Blockchain for Transparency

Learn how blockchain technology can reduce corruption and increase governmental transparency. Based on the Estonian e-governance model.

Target audience: Tech enthusiasts, policy makers, anti-corruption advocates

Horizontal Organization in Practice

How to build effective networks without hierarchies. Practical skills for collaborative decision-making and distributed coordination.

Format: Interactive workshop with real-world exercises
