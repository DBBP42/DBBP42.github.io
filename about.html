About DBBP

The Society of Bright Beings of the Pan-Galaxy (DBBP) is a horizontal network of Slovenian citizens united by a vision: to transform our small but mighty nation into a model of progressive, evidence-based reform.

Founded on the principles of open collaboration, transparency, and a healthy appreciation for Douglas Adams' cosmic wisdom, we believe that meaningful change comes not from top-down hierarchies, but from engaged citizens working together.

Our 9-Point Reform Vision

We advocate for comprehensive reforms across nine key areas of society. Each proposal is grounded in research, international best practices, and a commitment to human dignity.

1. Universal Basic Income - 600€/month

The Foundation of Freedom

Every adult citizen receives €369/month unconditionally, with €190/month for students aged 16+. This ensures dignified living, reduces poverty, and enables innovation and creativity.

Financing: Recovered corruption assets, legalized cannabis taxation, bureaucracy savings, and water/energy exports.

Expected outcome: Unemployment below 2%, youth retention, GDP increase of 3-5% in the first year.

2. 30-Hour Work Week

Life Beyond the Office

Progressive transition: 35 hours by 2027, 30 hours by 2030 (or six 5-hour days - worker's choice). Unified contribution rate of 25% regardless of employment status.

Supporting infrastructure: State robotics factory "Slovenija Robotics" for automation of dangerous work.

Expected outcome: 50% reduction in burnout, 2-3% increase in productivity.

3. Energy & Water Self-Sufficiency

Sustainable Abundance

NEK extension to 2043 + NEK 2 construction (1,200 MW) = 130% electricity self-sufficiency. First 300 kWh/month free per household. Slovenia has the 2nd best water reserves in the EU.

Water exports: €3-5 billion annually from excess drinking water.

Timeline: NEK 2 construction begins 2028, full self-sufficiency by 2030.

4. Housing Reform

A Home for Everyone Under 35

Location bonus of €200-500/month until age 35 for rent or mortgage. State platform slavija-stanovanja.si replaces Airbnb with 0% commission - 100% stays in Slovenia.

Goal: 100,000 new housing units by 2030, 80% reduction in homelessness.

5. Substance Reform & Spirituality

End Prohibition, Begin Education

Natural substances (cannabis, psilocybin, ayahuasca): Full legalization, up to 12 plants at home.

Medical substances (LSD, MDMA, ketamine): Licensed therapy with individualized approaches.

Dangerous substances (GHB, fentanyl): State control similar to weapons.

Impact: Mafia loses €300-500 million annually. Evidence shows 79% depression remission with psilocybin, 67% PTSD resolution with MDMA.

6. Mental Health Revolution

Healing the Mind

€200 million annual fund for young researchers under 35. Individual approach - all methods that work are permitted, including psychedelic-assisted therapies.

Free access: Psilocybin for seniors 65+ (micro and ceremonial doses).

Goal: 50% reduction in depression within 5 years, 90% success rate for PTSD, depression, and addiction treatment in 3-6 months.

7. Criminal Justice Reform

Rehabilitation Over Revenge

Transform prisons into clinics for the soul. Voluntary psychedelic therapy reduces recidivism to under 10%. Successful therapy can reduce sentences by up to 70%.

Alternative sentences: Reforestation (100 hectares of new forests annually), work with elderly, public apologies + compensation.

Impact: 40% reduction in prison population.

8. Economic Boom & Balkan Union

Cooperation Creates Prosperity

+5-8% GDP in first year through tourism and exports. Union with Croatia and Montenegro by 2030 - shared market of 6.5 million people, 130% food self-sufficiency, 200% energy, unlimited water.

Cannabis economy: Commission 420 (youth under 35), home cultivation of 12 plants, state green shops - all revenue to UBI fund.

Goal: GDP per capita €65,000 (from current €38,000).

9. Fundamental Rights & Ethics

Rights for Individuals, Not Groups

Rights belong to every human being, regardless of gender, orientation, origin, or past. Protection for system victims (LGBT+, migrants, disabled persons).

Ethics: Blockchain for transparency (Estonia model: -30% corruption).

Our Philosophy

We believe in horizontal organization - no leaders, just coordinators. We value evidence over ideology, compassion over punishment, and cooperation over competition. And we believe that even in an absurd universe, we can build something beautiful together.

"For instance, on the planet Earth, man had always assumed that he was more intelligent than dolphins because he had achieved so much—the wheel, New York, wars and so on—whilst all the dolphins had ever done was muck about in the water having a good time. But conversely, the dolphins had always believed that they were far more intelligent than man—for precisely the same reasons."
