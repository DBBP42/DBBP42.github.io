Welcome to the Pan-Galaxy

Greetings, fellow space traveler! You've stumbled upon the digital outpost of DBBP (Društvo Bistroumnih Bitij Pan-galaksije) - the Society of Bright Beings of the Pan-Galaxy.

We are a horizontal network of citizens dedicated to transforming Slovenia into a beacon of progressive reform, inspired by the spirit of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy. Because if we're going to navigate the complexities of modern society, we might as well do it with a towel in hand and a sense of cosmic perspective.

Our Mission

In a universe that's mostly harmless, we believe Slovenia can be exceptionally harmless - in the best way possible. We advocate for:

  • Basic Universal Income - Because everyone deserves the freedom to explore their potential without the existential dread of poverty
  • 30-Hour Work Week - Life is too short to spend it all in the office (especially when you could be reading Douglas Adams)
  • Energy & Water Self-Sufficiency - Sustainable resources for a sustainable future
  • Housing Reform - A home for every being by age 35
  • Mental Health Revolution - Evidence-based approaches to healing and wellness
  • Criminal Justice Reform - Rehabilitation over punishment
  • Economic Growth & Balkan Unity - Because cooperation beats competition
  • Human Rights for All - Every individual matters, regardless of origin or identity

Why "Pan-Galaxy"?

"The Answer to the Great Question... Of Life, the Universe and Everything... Is... Forty-two."

We embrace the absurdist wisdom of The Hitchhiker's Guide because it reminds us that even in the face of cosmic insignificance, we can choose to build something meaningful. Our "pan-galactic" vision isn't about space travel (yet) - it's about thinking beyond borders, beyond conventional limits, and beyond the tired old systems that no longer serve humanity.

Get Involved

Whether you're a concerned citizen, a policy enthusiast, or just someone who appreciates a good towel joke, there's a place for you in DBBP. We're building a movement based on:

  • Evidence-based policy proposals
  • Transparent governance
  • Horizontal organization (no hierarchies, just humans)
  • Open collaboration
  • A healthy sense of humor about the absurdity of existence

Check out our About page to learn more about our detailed reform proposals, join us at our events, or get in touch to become part of the network.

Remember: Don't Panic, but do participate.
